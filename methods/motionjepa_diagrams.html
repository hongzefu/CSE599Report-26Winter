Figure · MotionJEPA training objective. Paired RGB frames It, It+Δ are tokenized by a single frozen SigLIP backbone (shared weights) into patch-token grids xt, xt+Δ. The trainable MotionEncoder fθ compresses the visual delta together with the proprio delta Δst into a length-M motion code mt. A diffusion-style timestep k and the current tokens xt condition MotionDiT gφ, a training-only decoder whose zero-init residual head predicts the (TokenNorm-standardized) future tokens; the L2 regression ℒpred is the only signal that drives gradients back into fθ. At deployment gφ is discarded — only mt = fθ(xt, xt+Δ, Δst) is exposed to the downstream policy.
